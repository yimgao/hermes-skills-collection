# Pitch Deck Helper

> Structure a winning pitch deck. Guides you through each slide: what to say, what data to include, and how to tell your story for investors.

---

## When to Use

- *"Help me structure my pitch deck for investors"*
- *"What slides should be in a Series A deck?"*
- *"Review my pitch deck structure"*
- *"I'm raising $500K — help me prepare"*

---

## Core Workflow

### Step 1: Define Stage

| Stage | Deck Length | Focus |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| Pre-seed | 8-10 slides | Problem + Team |
| Seed | 10-12 slides | Traction + Market |
| Series A | 12-15 slides | Metrics + Growth |
| Series B+ | 15-20 slides | Scale + Unit economics |

### Step 2: Generate Slide Structure

```
1. Title — name, tagline, your name
2. Problem — the pain point (1 sentence)
3. Solution — what you built (demo / screenshot)
4. Market size — TAM/SAM/SOM (market-sizing skill)
5. Why now — timing / trend
6. Product — how it works (3 key features)
7. Traction — revenue, users, growth (chart)
8. Competition — matrix (you vs them)
9. Business model — how you make money
10. Team — why you're the right people
11. Financials — 3-year projection
12. Ask — how much + what for
```

### Step 3: Per-Slide Tips

```markdown
## Slide 2: Problem
❌ "Many people struggle with X"
✅ "35M Americans spend $12B/year on X, and the current options are broken"

**Data to include:**
- Statistic that quantifies the pain
- A quote from a real customer (if available)
```
