# Brand Voice Generator

> Analyze a brand's communication to produce a brand voice guide. Examines website copy, social media, and product messaging.

---

## When to Use

- *"Analyze Stripe's brand voice"*
- *"Create a brand voice guide for my startup"*
- *"How does this competitor communicate?"*
- *"Help me define my brand's tone"*

---

## Core Workflow

### Step 1: Collect Samples

Browse 3-5 pages of the brand's content:
- Homepage / landing page
- Blog posts
- Social media (Twitter/LinkedIn)
- Product copy / documentation
- About page / mission statement

### Step 2: Analyze Dimensions

| Dimension | What to Look For | Examples |
|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| **Tone** | Formal / Casual / Playful / Authoritative | "Welcome" vs "Hey there" |
| **Vocabulary** | Jargon level, word choices, metaphors | Industry terms vs plain English |
| **Sentence structure** | Short/punchy vs long/narrative | "Ship fast." vs "We believe in..." |
| **Pronouns** | We/You/I vs passive | "We build" vs "It is built" |
| **Humor** | Yes/No, type of humor | Puns, self-deprecating, none |
| **Emoji use** | Frequency, style | ✅ sparingly vs 🎉🚀🔥 |

### Step 3: Generate Voice Guide

```markdown
# Brand Voice Guide: {Brand}

## Personality
{3 adjectives}: e.g., "Confident, Clear, Human"

## Tone Spectrum
| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Marketing | {e.g., Bold, aspirational} |
| Support | {e.g., Warm, helpful} |
| Documentation | {e.g., Clear, concise} |
| Social Media | {e.g., Witty, conversational} |

## Do's
- {Do 1}
- {Do 2}

## Don'ts
- {Don't 1}
- {Don't 2}

## Example Phrasing
| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use Instead |
|----------|---------------|
| {bad example} | {good example} |
```
